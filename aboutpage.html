About RaktSetu

Connecting Lives Through Digital Blood Banking

Our Mission

To revolutionize blood donation and distribution through digital innovation, ensuring timely access to safe blood for everyone in need.

"At RaktSetu, we believe that no one should lose their life due to lack of blood. Our digital platform bridges the gap between donors and recipients, making blood donation more accessible, efficient, and impactful."

RaktSetu is a pioneering digital blood bank platform that leverages technology to streamline the blood donation process. We're building a robust network connecting donors, hospitals, and patients to ensure that blood reaches those who need it most, when they need it most.

Our Vision

To create a world where every person has timely access to safe blood, powered by a community-driven digital ecosystem that makes giving and receiving blood seamless.

Our Values

Compassion, Innovation, Reliability, Transparency, and Community. These core values guide every aspect of our work and shape our approach to revolutionizing blood banking.

Our Journey

From concept to reality, follow our path as we build RaktSetu from the ground up.

January 2025

Concept Development

Ananya and Suryansh identified the critical gaps in blood banking systems and conceptualized RaktSetu as a digital solution to bridge these gaps.

January 2025

Team Formation

The founding team was established, bringing together expertise in healthcare, technology, and operations to build a comprehensive digital blood banking platform.

January - May 2025

Platform Development

Development of the RaktSetu platform began in January and was completed in May, focusing on creating a user-friendly interface for donors, recipients, and healthcare providers.
